# What is a Business?

## What makes a Buisness?

It is a decision-making organisation that is involved in the process of using inputs to produce goods and/ or to provide services.

<Card type="definition">

### Products are used to refer to goods and services

* Goods are physical products
* Services are intangible products
* Businesses can also provide goods and services to other organisations (Example: distribution, freight transportation)

</Card>
